2ï¸âƒ£ Mobile Apps ka AsyncStorage kya hota hai?

Ye asynchronous hota hai, yani ye Promise return karta hai aur await/then use karna padta hai.
Example:

âœ… Features:
âœ”ï¸ Mobile Apps me kaam karta hai (React Native ke liye best option).
âœ”ï¸ Large data store kar sakta hai (Compared to localStorage).

âŒ Limitations:
âŒ Async nature hone ki wajah se await ya .then() use karna padta hai.
âŒ LocalStorage jitna fast nahi hota (kyunki har baar Promise resolve karna padta hai).
